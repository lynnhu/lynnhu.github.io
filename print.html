Screen-printed posters

At Glow I was also responsible for planning design team building events. For two of the teambuilding we went to IdleBeats Print Club, an independent screenprinting space/studio in Shanghai. I designed two posters based on two of Glow’s culture values in Adobe Illustrator then screen-printed them myself.
